Climate

These tortoises live in barren, arid and rocky environments with very low rainfall. They are commonly found in steppes, deserts, hillsides, and grasslands. In these harsh, arid environments, the weather can be extreme. When it gets too hot or too cold, the tortoises will retreat to their burrows for months at a time.
